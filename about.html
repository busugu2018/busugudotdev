About Me

Bonjour et bienvenue sur ma page!

My name is Nziemaraang (Dziae-mah-rong), a Gabonese software developer based in Dallas, Texas. My passion for technology began at the age of six with my Nintendo 64, evolved at 13 when I started producing music using software like Logic Pro and FL Studio, and continues today through creating websites and integrating APIs.

I bring 6 years of experience across all phases of the software development lifecycle, including requirements gathering, technical analysis, design, development, integration, testing, and implementation. My expertise lies in building and maintaining microservices APIs, with extensive experience developing Mule ESB flows and integrations using Service-Oriented Architecture (SOA) and Enterprise Application Integration (EAI) principles on Mule runtimes.
